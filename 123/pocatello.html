The Historic City of Pocatello

Pocatello was founded in 1889, and was named after an Indian chief of the Shoshone tribe. It was founded in large part due to settlers during the gold rush. Idaho State University was founded in 1901, and the town of Pocatello has grown immensely during that time. Pocatello is home to 55,525 people according to a census taken in 2019, and boasts a large outdoors community. There are bike paths and mountains only a short drive away, and the beautiful scenery makes Pocatello a place to remember. Historic Old Town hosts many events during the summer, from a weekly Farmer's Market to parades and art shows. There is definitely something for everyone in Pocatello.
