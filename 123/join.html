Membership Options

  • Gold: The Gold Membership includes free access to community events for setting up a business stand and shoutouts on our home page.
  • Silver: The Silver Membership includes discounts to community events for setting up a business stand.
  • Bronze: The Bronze Membership allows you to register for a stand at community events to promote your business.
  • Non-Profit: For Non-Profits only, includes everything in the Silver Membership.
