Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to allow people to check the weather wherever they are or are going and to plan accordingly. It will plan for the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven It will also feature images that reflect the weather in those towns and provide a report on the weather. In addition, there will be a gallery that people can post pictures of weather landscapes to. There will also be a storm watch page to help users be aware of incoming storms. Overall, the purpose is to allow people to be know what the weather will be like for their trips and is designed for anyone in the area.
