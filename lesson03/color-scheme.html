Color Scheme

This is my color scheme for the Weather Outside Now site. I will use an off-white color for text against darker background (rgb(228, 228, 228)) and use black against lighter backgrounds. The blues are found in my header and footer, while the yellow and orange are more of accent colors used for A tags and break lines.
