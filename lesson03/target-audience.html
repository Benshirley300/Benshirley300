Target Audience

The target audience for this website is people who live or is traveling to one of these three cities. They can be any age, though more likely to be adults. As such, this page has a clean appearance to attract busy adults who need the information they are looking for quickly. The gallery is for people who have a bit more time to spend on the site.

Personas

Miranda: A young mom with 2 kids and 2 dogs who lives in Preston. There is a park about 15 minutes away where she loves to take her kids and dogs in the afternoons, but only if it is good weather outside. Uses the site mostly on her phone as a quick way to view the weather, but also likes viewing the gallery on her iPad.

Brad: Lives in Rexburg but often works out in Soda Springs. He loves to take his bike down with him when he works and goes biking during his breaks. Checks the weather on the drive down on his smartphone to see if he will be able bike today.


Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A man working in Fish Haven who wants to know if he should take his truck due to snow or can take his car.
  • Scenario 2: An older lady who loves pictures of beautiful landscapes and weather patterns.
  • Scenario 3: A teenager who lives in Preston who wants to go hiking.
  • Scenario 4: An event coordinator planning a town picnic at a local park who wants to plan it for a sunny day.
  • Scenario 5: A mom planning a graduation for her son and wants to ensure good weather.
