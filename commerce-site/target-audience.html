Target Audience

The target audience for this site is customers who shop in Pocatello Idaho. This site contains links to local businesses that are registered with the Chamber of Commerce, as well as contact info and location information. This site is also for businesses who are looking to network with other businesses and become involved in the community for greater consumer exposure.

Personas

Miranda: A young mom with 2 children who loves going to events that local businesses put on. She also is a big supporter of local businesses and does most of her shopping at local stores.

Brad: A new business owner who is looking to gain community exposure by becoming involved with the Chamber of Commerce. He hosts several job fairs for careers in computers and wants to help more people know about these events.


Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: An older couple who loves the community and wants to participate in local events.
  • Scenario 2: A young woman who operates her business out of her home and wants to get more foot traffic.
  • Scenario 3: An established business owner who wants to boost the economy in the area.
  • Scenario 4: A city councillor who wants to network with local businesses for campaign sponsorship.
  • Scenario 5: A college graduate looking for local businesses to pitch a business idea to.
