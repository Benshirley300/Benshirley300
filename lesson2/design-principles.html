Design Principles

Ben Shirley

White Space and Clean Design

Google

Google's home page is a good example of white space and clean design. THis page keeps a simple design, with the logo, a search bar, and a news article being the most prominent. There is plenty of white space which helps to keep the viewer's attention on the important aspects of the page.

Visual Hierarchy

O'Reilly Auto Parts

O'Reilly Auto Parts' home page is a good example of visual hierarchy. The first thing that a viewer notices is something that O'Reilly wants to sell, followed by some key marketing words. Next the viewer's eyes will migrate to the O'Reilly logo and selecting a store/shopping by a vehicle.

Alignment

BYU-I

BYU-I's mobile website is a good example of alignment. Each of the elements on the page are not touching the edges, and there is an equal amount of space between the text and each of the boxes that the text is in. The viewer's eyes will move gently down the page instead of going back and forth looking for the beginning of each line.
